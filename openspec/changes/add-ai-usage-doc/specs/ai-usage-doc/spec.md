## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: AI Usage Documentation
The project SHALL provide an AI usage documentation that explains how AI was used and what decisions were made by human.

#### Scenario: Document AI assistance in key areas
- **WHEN** evaluating AI assistance
- **THEN** documentation shows where AI provided critical help

#### Scenario: Document unaccepted AI suggestions
- **WHEN** AI suggested overly complex solutions
- **THEN** documentation explains why they were rejected

#### Scenario: Document human-made decisions
- **WHEN** making critical engineering decisions
- **THEN** documentation explains the rationale and human judgment
